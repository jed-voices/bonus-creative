# Bonus Creative Brand Brief

## Overview
Bonus Creative needs a brand-new, premium, ultra-fast website for an Oklahoma-based video producer. The site should feel more like a modern film studio or high-end creative director portfolio than a typical local production-company site.

This is a filmmaker-led brand. The website must communicate that clients are working directly with one experienced creator who can carry a project from concept to final cut.

## Core positioning
The site should clearly communicate:

- one creator
- one vision
- direct collaboration
- cinematic quality
- fast, lean execution
- story-first thinking
- premium results without a bloated agency process

## Brand promise
Clients get a calm, direct, story-first process with premium execution and real access to the filmmaker doing the work.

## Tone
- confident
- calm
- cinematic
- concise
- intelligent
- human
- grounded

Avoid hype, fluff, buzzwords, and generic agency language.

## Audience
Primary audiences include:

- nonprofits
- mission-driven organizations
- churches
- brands
- founders
- conferences and event clients
- corporate teams that want a filmmaker, not a large agency

## Visual direction
- cinematic
- sophisticated
- dark, elegant, and minimal
- strong typography
- large visual rhythm
- restrained but bold
- stylish without gimmicks
- premium but not stiff
- emotional and story-driven

## What to avoid
- generic agency positioning
- cookie-cutter layouts
- overexplaining gear
- cluttered service sections
- heavy animations
- wedding-video clichés
- noisy hero videos
- outdated template feel

## Site objectives
1. Make visitors instantly feel the work is premium and credible
2. Explain the strategic value of direct collaboration
3. Surface the reel, selected work, and case studies quickly
4. Move visitors toward an inquiry or discovery call
5. Stay exceptionally fast on desktop and mobile
