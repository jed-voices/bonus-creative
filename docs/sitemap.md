# Sitemap

## Primary pages

### 1. Home
Purpose: establish taste, credibility, and conversion path immediately.

Recommended sections:
- Hero
- Trust strip
- Why direct collaboration works
- Featured work
- Services overview
- Case study preview
- About preview
- Testimonials or proof
- Final CTA

### 2. Work
Purpose: show selected films quickly and elegantly.

Recommended structure:
- Intro statement
- Filterable or grouped project grid
- Project cards with category, still frame, and brief outcome line
- Links into project detail pages

### 3. Services
Purpose: explain what Bonus Creative makes and why the process is valuable.

Recommended service groups:
- Branded films
- Documentary and story-driven content
- Nonprofit storytelling
- Church media
- Event and convention coverage
- Drone cinematography
- Production partner or shooter-editor for hire

### 4. About
Purpose: humanize the founder and explain the creative edge.

Recommended sections:
- Founder portrait
- Short story
- Awards or recognition
- How the filmmaker works
- Industries served
- Why clients choose direct access

### 5. Case Studies
Purpose: show strategic thinking, not just visual output.

Recommended structure:
- Index page with selected case studies
- Individual project detail pages

### 6. Contact
Purpose: make inquiry dead simple and high trust.

Recommended structure:
- Short intro
- Simple inquiry form
- Project type dropdown
- Timeline dropdown
- Budget range optional
- Email, phone, and social links

## Optional pages
- Reel
- FAQ
- Journal or Insights
