# Mobile-First Interaction Notes

## General principles
- Keep the first screen immediately clear
- Make CTA buttons obvious and thumb-friendly
- Avoid heavy background media on mobile
- Preserve visual drama without slowing the page down

## Hero
- Prioritize headline, short statement, and CTA
- Use a still image or poster frame instead of heavy autoplay video on mobile
- Keep the first section readable in under three seconds

## Work grid
- Use strong thumbnails and short captions
- Avoid dense metadata
- Keep tap targets generous

## Services
- Stack services cleanly
- Use concise descriptions
- Do not overwhelm with long paragraphs

## Contact
- Keep the form short
- Use native mobile-friendly fields and dropdowns
- Make email and phone links one tap

## Motion
- Keep hover effects subtle on desktop and nonessential on mobile
- Use motion only when it improves hierarchy or feedback
